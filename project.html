Christmas Kindness Advent Calendar

Author: Soojin Lee     Date: 10, Dec, 2021

| Christmas Kindness Advent Calendar

It is the time of the year where you start hearing Jingles and see Christmas Decorations on the street. As the Christmas approaches, I made this project hoping to make the community around me, a kinder place to live in. With a little creativity, you can share "heart-warming" moments to people around you and Christmas Advent Calendar makes it easier for you. With Christmas Advent Calendar, receive a randomly generated kindness prompts everyday, and practice them to make your and people around your Christmas a warmer one.

| User-testing and Feedback

On December 1st, I proposed my final project idea and the working prototype to the class. Here is the feedback I received.
  • On click, add Christmas Jingle to make it more festive.
  • Consider using getDate(); to only allow the access on the specific date.
  • Allow people send, and save challenge prompt to their email.
  • Collect people's achievements / feedback

| UX/UI Design

Save Prompt

Using Save Prompt function, users can save the daily challenge to their own computer device. If they press the button, it will automatically save the prompt as a txt file. Moving on, I would like to add a feature where users log-in to access this advent calendar so that save-prompt will send the txt to users' inbox.

Renew

The whole purpose of this challenge is to encourage and incentivize people to spread the act of kindness, not to force, or punish. Therefore, if the user wants to receive a new prompt, they can click renew button to receive a new challenge prompt.

Done!

Once users have completed, the button is no longer functional. Instead, the button will change to Christmas Green color with a text that says "Done".

| Reflection + Moving Forward

Intent VS Impact

While this project is intended to share kindness, not to scare or make others feel uncomfortable, some of the prompt may yield the unwanted impact.

Next Steps

  • Instead of saving prompt as txt file, allow users to send challenge prompt to their email or any social media account they wish.
  • Add an array of Jingle sound and select randomly so find a Christmas Sound API to provide different Jingle sound everyday for the users.
  • Share project with friends and family around me.
